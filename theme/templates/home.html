Workshops

DSI offers regular workshops, typically each week. These are mostly at the intermediate and advanced for common topics (e.g., R, git, python) and also introduces more exotic topics. Contact us if you want to teach a workshop. Vote to tell us which topics interest you most.

Propose workshop topics

Problem Solving Un-seminars

DSI runs a series of novel "un-seminars". These are highly interdisciplinary brain storming sessions to solve interesting data science problems in research projects from any discipline. The speakers are typically not data scientists, but are considering or grappling with a project that has a tricky data science problem. Speakers describe the data science problem in 10 - 15 minutes and then the audience asks more questions and offers suggestions and possible solutions. These are very valuable and informative for all participants and a terrific place to learn about the breadth of data science applications and solutions.

Contact us if you are interested in presenting in this series.

Call for DSI affiliates

The Data Science Initiative (DSI) is building a campus community interested in Data Science. Graduate students and postdocs developing and/or applying data science methods to research problems can apply to be affiliated with the DSI.
