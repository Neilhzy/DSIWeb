Workshops

DSI offers regular workshops, typically each week. These are mostly at the intermediate and advanced for common topics (e.g., R, git, python) and also introduces more exotic topics. Contact us if you want to teach a workshop. Vote to tell us which topics interest you most.

Propose workshop topics

Problem Solving Un-seminars

DSI runs a series of novel "un-seminars" - highly interdisciplinary brain storming sessions to solve interesting data science problems in research projects from any discipline. Speakers, typically not data scientists, are grappling with a project that involves a non-trivial data science problem. They describe the data science problem in 10-15 minutes and then the audience asks more questions and offers suggestions and possible solutions. This is a very valuable and informative venue for speaker and audience alike, and a terrific place to learn about the breadth of data science applications and solutions. Contact us if you are interested in presenting in this series.

Call for DSI affiliates

The Data Science Initiative (DSI) is building a campus community interested in Data Science. Graduate students and postdocs developing and/or applying data science methods to research problems can apply to be affiliated with the DSI.
